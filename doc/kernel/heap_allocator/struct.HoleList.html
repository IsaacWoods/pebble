pub unsafe fn free(&mut self, ptr: *mut u8, layout: Layout)

Free an allocation defined by ptr and layout. Unsafe because if ptr was not returned by a call to allocate_first_fit, undefined behaviour may occur. Deallocates by walking the list and inserts the given hole at the correct position. If the freed block is adjacent to another one, they are merged.
